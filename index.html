Our Heritage

A Family Legacy Since 1968

Pita Cigars began with one man's hands and a bundle of Cuban-seed tobacco — our founder, rolling cigars in Miami the same way he learned as a young man. What started as a family trade has become a name trusted by cigar lovers across South Florida and beyond.

Today, that same recipe lives on. Every Pita cigar is still hand-rolled in small batches, using Nicaraguan long-filler tobacco and the exact techniques passed down through our family — nothing shortcut, nothing outsourced.

The Distinction of Excellence

  • 🌿

    Hand-Selected Tobacco

    Every leaf personally inspected by our master blenders before entering the aging room.

  • 🏅

    Award-Winning Blends

    Recognized globally with over 40 ratings of 93+ points from leading cigar publications.

  • 🌎

    World-Sourced Tobaccos

    Wrapper, binder, and filler sourced from the finest growing regions across 12 countries.

  • 🔥

    Slow Cured Wrappers

    Wrapper leaves fermented for a minimum of 18 months to achieve a smooth, even burn.
