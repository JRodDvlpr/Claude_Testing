The Distinction of Excellence

  • 🌿

    Hand-Selected Tobacco

    Every leaf personally inspected by our master blenders before entering the aging room.

  • 🏅

    Award-Winning Blends

    Recognized globally with over 40 ratings of 93+ points from leading cigar publications.

  • 🌎

    World-Sourced Tobaccos

    Wrapper, binder, and filler sourced from the finest growing regions across 12 countries.

  • 🔥

    Slow Cured Wrappers

    Wrapper leaves fermented for a minimum of 18 months to achieve a smooth, even burn.

  • ⌛

    Aged to Perfection

    All blends rest in Spanish cedar humidors for a minimum of three years before release.

  • 🏅

    Master Blenders Only

    No production shortcuts. Every cigar passes through the hands of a certified master roller.
