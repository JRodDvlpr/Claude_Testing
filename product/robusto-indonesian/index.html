The Blend

Crafted Leaf by Leaf

The Robusto Indonesian drapes our Nicaraguan long-filler in an Indonesian-grown wrapper, a darker, oilier leaf that trades the Sumatra’s sweetness for depth.

Expect a fuller, earthier smoke with notes of dark cocoa and seasoned oak, balanced so the strength builds gently rather than announcing itself. The same blend beneath, an entirely different evening.
