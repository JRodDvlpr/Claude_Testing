The Blend

Crafted Leaf by Leaf

The Toro Habano is the everyday favorite of the Pita range, a straight, generously sized toro under a sun-grown Habano wrapper grown in the fields of Estélí.

Its aged Nicaraguan filler gives a medium body with remarkable complexity: dried fruit and cinnamon woven through a foundation of oak and cocoa. A cigar meant to be savored without occasion.
